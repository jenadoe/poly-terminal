Methodology note

What the labels mean

Strata evaluates how a market price should be handled as a Polymarket reference based on public market structure, wording, resolution sensitivity, option context, and timing risk.

  • Ready - Cite with source: use the price directly with a Polymarket link and timestamp/source context.
  • Context Required - Attach context: keep the specific option, wording, timing, or resolution detail beside the price.
  • Review Recommended - Check rules first: inspect market rules, resolution criteria, timing, or sensitivity before reuse.
  • Not Standalone - Do not quote alone: use only with full market wording or surrounding explanation.

Example: "2026 NBA Champion at 36%" loses the priced team. "Polymarket prices New York Knicks to win the 2026 NBA Finals at 36%" keeps the selected option attached, so the reference is much easier to reuse responsibly.

Boundary note

What this does not mean

A Strata label is not a forecast, endorsement, odds validation, or investment recommendation. It only describes whether the market price can be reused as a Polymarket reference without losing important structure or context.

  • No raw trades, wallets, or participant identifiers are shown.
  • No numeric rating is used as a public-facing claim.
  • No label should be interpreted as trading or outcome guidance.
