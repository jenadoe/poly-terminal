Methodology
How It Works
A continuously running pipeline measuring structural integrity of prediction market prices — not their outcomes.
01
Market Data Collection

Every 30 minutes, active Polymarket events are fetched and validated. For top markets by volume, live order book depth is retrieved directly from the CLOB API — capturing real bid/ask liquidity rather than nominal volume. Macro signals (BTC, SPY, rates) are tracked for correlation analysis.

Polymarket API CLOB order book Macro signals Pydantic validation
02
4-Dimension Integrity Scoring

Each market is scored across four independent dimensions that together determine whether a price reflects genuine crowd consensus or structural noise.

Capital
Depth and quality of market liquidity
Efficiency
Spread tightness and price stability
Independence
Insulation from macro market movements
Convergence
Consensus lock-in strength over time
03
State Classification

The Nexus Score (0–100) maps each market to a structural state using category-specific thresholds, a confirmation window, and hysteresis to prevent false transitions.

Converged Structural consensus locked. Reliable pricing signal.
Calibrating Price discovery in progress. Consensus still forming.
Fragile Structural weakness detected. Treat price signal with caution.
