Methodology
How It Works
A continuously running pipeline measuring structural integrity of prediction market prices — not their outcomes.
01
Continuous Data Collection

Active Polymarket events are fetched and validated continuously. For each market, real liquidity depth and macro market signals are captured — not just nominal trading volume.

02
Structural Integrity Scoring

Each market is evaluated across multiple structural dimensions and assigned a NXS score from 0 to 100. This measures the reliability of the market's price signal — not the probability of any outcome.

03
State Classification

Each market is classified into one of three structural states.

Converged Structural consensus locked. Reliable pricing signal.
Calibrating Price discovery in progress. Consensus still forming.
Fragile Structural weakness detected. Price signal unreliable.
