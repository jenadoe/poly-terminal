Methodology note

What the labels mean

Strata evaluates reference posture based on public market structure, market wording, resolution sensitivity, and context risk. The status describes whether a price can stand on its own or needs additional framing.

  • Ready: cite with standard source attribution. This is not a claim that the market price is correct.
  • Context Required: cite only with the stated context attached to the price.
  • Review Recommended: check wording, resolution source, or timing before citing.
  • Not Standalone: do not present the price by itself; use only inside broader explanation.
Boundary note

What this does not mean

A Strata label is not a forecast, endorsement, odds validation, or investment recommendation. It only describes whether the market price needs additional framing before it appears in another surface.

  • No raw trades, wallets, or participant identifiers are shown.
  • No numeric rating is used as a public-facing claim.
  • No label should be interpreted as trading or outcome guidance.
Feedback ask

Three things we want reviewers to judge

We are testing whether this format fits real reference, research, dashboard, and AI-output workflows. Useful feedback is specific and operational.

  1. Did the status match how you would handle this market price?
  2. Was the suggested reference text usable, too cautious, or too generic?
  3. Which market or workflow would you want Strata to cover next?
